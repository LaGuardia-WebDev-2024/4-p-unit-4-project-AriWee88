"Ayamonte. La pesca del atún (1919)"

This piece is one of fourteen within his mural series called "Vision of Spain". The murals are situated in the American Hispanic Society in New York!!

These are other examples of his work. His work has a lot of beach scenes, spanish life, and the human figure. He is also known as the 'master of light' as much of his work puts emphasis on light.
The first one, Fisherwomen, 1911, is one of my favorites!
